ℹ️ About NeuralCare

Your personal mental health companion

What is NeuralCare?
NeuralCare is an AI-powered mental health support platform designed specifically for India. It provides immediate, affordable, and stigma-free emotional support to everyone.

Key Features:
• 24/7 AI Chat Support
• Mood Tracking & Insights
• Personal Journal
• Medication Reminders
• Daily Routine Builder
• Crisis Support

Supported Languages:
English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, Malayalam

📖 How to Use

1. AI Chat - Talk to our friendly AI about anything. It understands emotions and responds naturally.

2. Mood Tracker - Track your daily mood. AI automatically detects emotions from chat.

3. Journal - Write private reflections. AI analyzes sentiment.

4. Medications - Add medicines and get email reminders.

5. Daily Routine - Build healthy habits with reminders.

6. Crisis Support - Click "Crisis" button for emergency help.

🔔 Notifications

Manage your reminders and alerts

Email Notifications: You'll receive email reminders for:
• Medication times
• Daily routine tasks
• Weekly mood summaries

In-App Notifications: Click the 🔔 bell icon on any page to see all your reminders.
